# Pacman Game

Use the coordinates to move Pacman by creating an issue.

## How to Play

1. Create a new issue.
2. In the issue body, specify the coordinates where you want Pacman to move in the format:
      X: [column number]
      Y: [row number]
3. Submit the issue.

## Game Board

P: Pacman
W: Wall
. : Dot

| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| P | . | W | . | . | . | W | . |
| . | W | W | W | . | W | W | . |
| . | . | . | . | . | . | . | . |
| . | W | W | W | W | W | W | . |
| . | . | . | . | . | . | . | . |
| . | W | W | W | W | W | W | . |
| . | . | . | . | . | . | . | . |
| . | W | . | . | W | W | . | . |

Title: Move Pacman to (3, 1)

X: 3
